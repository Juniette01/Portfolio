An Introduction to Belly Dance

Data Visualization

Belly dance, a type of Middle Eastern dance, is an expressive and often improvisational dance that has historically been danced by women for other women. The true origin of the dance is unknown, but theories say that it may have originated from ritual dances or to help prepare the body for child birth. It is usually performed barefoot and with colorful costumes.

Its popularity continues to grow worldwide, but there are still some misunderstandings about the dance. I created this visualization as a quick introduction for viewers to learn the basics of belly dance and get familiar with some common rhythms and props.
